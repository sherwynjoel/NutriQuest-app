Nutrition Basics
Learn the fundamentals of nutrition and healthy eating
üçé What is Nutrition?
Nutrition is the process by which our bodies use food for energy, growth, and health. Good nutrition means eating a variety of foods that provide the nutrients your body needs.
‚ö° Macronutrients
The three main nutrients your body needs in large amounts: Carbohydrates (energy), Proteins (building blocks), and Fats (energy storage and cell function).
üíé Micronutrients
Vitamins and minerals your body needs in smaller amounts but are essential for health, growth, and disease prevention.
